About me

My name is Okoro Patrick Chidera, i am an igbo boy, i hail from ebonyi state of ohaozara local government. I am the fourth of the six children given birth in my family,I was born into a comfortable home, where my mother sells food and my late father sells machine spare parts. I was given birth in ikom local government area of cross river state, where i attended holy family primary school and obtained my first school leaving certificate. And relocated to asaba,delta state where i attended Honour roll international schools where i was given the senior prefect of the prestigious school. I was ten years old when i lost dad, that was really difficult for my family things became worst i had to drop from school due,to lack of funds. I started begging people around me just to feed things went sour for me i had to relocate to asaba to find greener pastures which i later found by the grace of God. in my family. We have two boys and four girls i have an elder brother who caters for me and my family he was the one who brought the idea of programming when i finished high school at first i said no because i thought programming was too difficult, so he persisted he got me a laptop to code so i started watching youtube tutorials, and really got fascinated about programming in general. I started learning amazing stuffs,building projects but i wasn't satisfied so i enrolled in altschoolafrica and alx africa!!! to brush of my coding skills i hope this would be an audacious journey cause i want to really build projects.
